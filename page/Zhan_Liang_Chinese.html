Overview
Meaning “Brilliant and shining light” in Chinese – Zhan Liang makes a debut as a unique Chinese dining experience in Cambodia. The design of this refined and authentic Chinese restaurant can be compared to any of Beijing, Shanghai, or Hong Kong’s best pioneering restaurants. So, you can expect the culinary journey displaying the best of Chinese cuisines, featured by high-quality ingredients, and highly personalized service.
